Internet Speed Analyzer is a privacy-focused utility app that helps you monitor and analyze your internet connection performance. Get real-time network insights, perform accurate speed tests, and understand your network configuration.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does the speed test work?

The app downloads and uploads test files of various sizes to measure your actual internet speed. It uses multiple file sizes (1MB to 50MB) for accurate results.

Why can't I see my WiFi network name?

Due to iOS privacy restrictions, the app may not be able to display your WiFi network name (SSID). This is normal and doesn't affect the app's functionality.

Does the app collect my data?

No! Internet Speed Analyzer is designed with privacy in mind. All processing happens locally on your device, and no data is sent to external servers.

How accurate are the speed test results?

The app performs real speed tests using multiple file sizes and provides weighted averages for the most accurate results possible on mobile devices.

What network information is displayed?

The app shows your IP address, subnet mask, router IP, DNS servers, and connection type (WiFi, Cellular, or None).
